## SECTION 9 — FAQ (SEO + objection-handling)

**Eyebrow:** FREQUENTLY ASKED

**H2:** The questions trustees ask before signing.

**Q: How long does a typical campus take?**
Depends on scale. A K-12 phase-1 (6–8 acres, 1.5L sq ft) typically delivers in 14–18 months. We commit to a date in writing before contract.

**Q: What's included in the single contract?**
Design, build, fit-out, certify. From soil testing to occupancy certificate. Excludes land acquisition, statutory fees paid to government bodies, and IT hardware procurement (we recommend partners but don't bill it).

**Q: What if our requirements change mid-project?**
Change requests are quoted within 7 days with revised cost and timeline impact. You approve in writing, we proceed. No verbal changes, no scope creep surprises.

**Q: What's your minimum project size?**
₹15Cr / 50,000 sq ft. Below that, the single-contract model doesn't deliver enough cost or time advantage for it to make sense for either of us.

**Q: Do you work outside India?**
Currently India only. We've evaluated GCC and Southeast Asia, but our compliance + crew model isn't ready to export yet.

**Q: What happens if you miss the contracted date?**
Liquidated damages clause kicks in — capped at a percentage of contract value. The exact terms are in your MSA. In `[REAL]`+ projects, we've never invoked it.

## VOICE CHECKLIST (for any future copy you write)

Before shipping any new copy, check:

- [ ] No buzzwords (innovative, scalable, world-class, best-in-class, synergy, leverage, holistic, end-to-end)
- [ ] Specific over general (real numbers, real timeframes, real names)
- [ ] Direct over hedged (no "we strive to", "we aim to", "we believe in")
- [ ] Accountable over aspirational (what we DO, not what we VALUE)
- [ ] Reads like one founder talking to another, not an agency talking to a search engine
